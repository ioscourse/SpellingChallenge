    Our project:

    Team Member: Tom Kinser

    App Title: Spelling Challenge

    App Description: Application which test students with spelling lists which are user inputted. App Target Audience: K-8 App Features: Input: User typed or OCR Scanning. Output: User prompted by audio that is either automatically synthesized or custom inputted by user. Sharing: Lists could be shared amoung students by email, 'bumping', or cloud. Network Requirements: Needed for word set-up, not for testing. Device Compatibility: Application could be run on iPad, IPhone, or iTouch. Feedback: Results could be provided to user, email, or a central 'top score' cloud location. Retest: Later tests of same list could be based on previous results (i.e. retest only incorrect words). Game: Could incorporate 'game' styles of play (crossword puzzles, word search, ...).
